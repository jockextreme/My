Key Features of Philippine Politics

Government Structure

The Philippines has a democratic government with three co-equal branches: executive, legislative, and judiciary.

Political Parties

Elections & Voting

Political History Timeline

1898

Declaration of Independence

The Philippines declared independence from Spanish colonial rule on June 12, 1898.

1935

Commonwealth Government

Establishment of the Commonwealth of the Philippines under Manuel L. Quezon.

1946

Independence from the US

The Philippines gained full independence from the United States on July 4, 1946.

1972-1981

Martial Law Era

Ferdinand Marcos declared martial law, leading to 14 years of authoritarian rule.

1986

People Power Revolution

Peaceful revolution that ousted Ferdinand Marcos and installed Corazon Aquino as president.
